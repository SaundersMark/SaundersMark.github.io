Supplementary metadata

The metadata collated for the feasibility study can be split into the following categories:

  • Provided by the BirdSoundsDenoising dataset:
    • ID
    • Dataset (train, validate and test)
  • Determined by inspecting the audio files and masks in the BirdSoundsDenoising dataset:
    • Recording length
    • Sample rate
    • Mask edges (high and low frequencies, start and end times)
    • Mask pixel count (original and resized)
  • Metadata extracted from xeno-canto
    • Bird species and common names
    • Location of recordings (country, location, latitude and longitude)
    • Type of call (e.g. song)
    • Recording quality (e.g. A, B, C)
    • Date of recording
    • Remarks on the recording
    • Equipment used
    • Whether the audio has been processed
    • Whether the recording has been deleted
  • BirdNET data
    • The birds species and probabilities recognised in the original audio and denoised audio
  • Some narrative comments
  • Dice score for an illustrative model run using 1,000 samples
